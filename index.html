What is this?

This is a tomato timer. It might resemble the famous "pomodoro timer", but it's not. The only similiarity between these two is that the puny "Pomodoro" stole our technique. That's all.

So what's the technique?

The idea behind the Tomato Technique is to break down all of your tasks into 25 minute time blocks. Between each time block, there is a five minute break. And after completing four Tomatos you take a longer breakâ€”usually 15 to 30 minutes.

How do I use it?

Press the button to start your session. The default parameter is 25 minutes, but you can change the setting however you wish, using the and buttons. Please be aware, that changing the length of any of the parameters resets the whole timer. Once the work phase is done, a sound follows, indicating the start of a break(length of which you can adjust, too), after which the timer goes back to the work phase(and you should, too). And so on.
